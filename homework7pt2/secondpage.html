My favorite story is Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir. It is about Ryland Grace, an amnesiac scientist who wakes up alone on a spaceship, discovering he is on a desperate, one-way mission to save Earth from an alien microorganism, "Astrophage," that is dimming the sun.
